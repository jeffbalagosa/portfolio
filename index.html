Arithmetic Generator

This app came out of wanting to build something to help support my daughter's new love for math. It came out better than I hoped, so I wanted to showcase it here. The app generates random addition and subtraction problems.

I gamiefied it by placing a streak counter on the app. The more in a row you got correct, the higher the number grows. It goes back to zero when you submit a wrong answer. She enjoys playing it!

Fallout 4 Plot Generator

My son is a gamer, like myself. He likes American RPG games. At the time he was really into Bethesda games, perticularly Skyrim and Fallout 4. I wanted to make something that'll show him a cool use case for coding. To help him pique his interest.

So I wrote this app to generate a sort of guide for a new playthrough for this game.

An interesting challenge, was that some followers were incompatible with the faction your character chose to side with. So I had to research and write a bunch of conditional statements to ensure the app picked factions appropriate with your main followers.

If you're a fan of the Franchise, you'll notice the Easter Egg I put in as a hover state.
